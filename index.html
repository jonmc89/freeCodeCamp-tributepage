Mark Zuckerberg

Here is Mark's Timline...

  • 2004: Launched "Thefacebook" at Harvard, expanded to other schools, moved to Palo Alto, met Peter Thiel
  • 2005: Faced allegations of idea theft, settled with 1.2 million Facebook shares and $20 million
  • 2007: Named in MIT Technology Review's TR35 list
  • 2009: Consulted former Netscape CFO Peter Currie
  • 2010: Reached 500 million users, recognized as Vanity Fair's most influential person
  • 2011: Shared management advice from Steve Jobs
  • 2012: Met Russian Prime Minister Dmitry Medvedev, compared Facebook's user base in Russia to VK.
  • 2013: Promoted cross-communication between social platforms, discussed global internet access at TechCrunch Disrupt
  • 2014: Keynote at Mobile World Congress, hosted Chinese politician Lu Wei
  • 2016: Named one of Business Insider's "Top 10 Business Visionaries"
  • 2017: Received honorary degree from Harvard
  • 2019: Announced plans for end-to-end encrypted system integration
  • 2020: Integrated chat systems for Instagram and Messenger
